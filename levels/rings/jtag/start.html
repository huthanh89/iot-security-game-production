In this mission, you verify the serial connectivity using UART GPIO pins and exploit the debug interface on RPi that was left open by the developer. For this mission, gather with your teammates and complete it together. Step 1: Connect the USB2Serial cable to the UART GPIO pins on the RPi. Step 2: Start a serial console session from a terminal emulator (115200 bps) into the RPi to explore the input banner for the flag. Step 3: Explore the banner on the login prompt to find the flag.
